### In `full-blown`, it is not `blow` as in wind (from Germanic <*blÄ“anÄ…>) nor `blow` as in `hit` (Germanic <*blewwanÄ…>), but `blow` as in `bloom` (Germanic <*blÅanÄ…>), as in Shakespeare's "the bud ere it be blown".
