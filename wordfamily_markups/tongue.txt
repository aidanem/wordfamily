### `Lingurari`=rom-Latn are an ethnic group of Romani, descended from enslaved Romani in Wallachia (now Romania, no etymological relation to Romani). The Lingurari were part of the Boyash or Rudari group that were forced to give up their nomadic lifestyle and work in mines. When the mines became depleted, many switched to making their living as wood-carvers, including the Lingurari or "Spoon-makers".
