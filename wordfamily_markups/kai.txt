startintro

A quick Austronesian word family inspired by my friend Kai's birthday: Malayo-Polynesian <*tasik>: "sea, coastal water, saltwater, tide" -> Hawaiʻian `kai`=haw-Latn: "sea, saltwater, coast, tide".

The Oceanic compound <*taqu-tasi>: "expert sailor" (lit. "sea-able, sea-knowing") is such a cool word, I included Austronesian <*tsaqu>: "to know how, to be able", too!

endintro
