startintro

There are two words from Sumerian whose descendants are still used all over the world today: [wff-cane|<gi>]: "reed"—what they wrote with—and <dub>: "tablet"—what they wrote on.

Just in case you wonder how important Sumerian writing was in world history.

The word `tablet`=eng-Latn (Latin `tabula`=lat-Latn) is probably not related, despite the phonological and semantic match. On the other hand, there's disagreement about exactly where `tabula`=lat-Latn does come from, so…


endintro
