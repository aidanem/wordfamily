startintro

Latin words `munio`=lat-Latn: "fortifiation" (English `munitions`=eng-Latn) and `murus`=lat-Latn: "wall" (English `mural`=eng-Latn) appear to come from a <*mey-> root, so could be related, but are more likely connected to <*mewtʰi-> which gives various words about "pole, stick, post, picket"

endintro
