# Old Latin `robus`=lat-Latn had an oblique stem of `robores`=lat-Latn due to intervocalic rhoticization of the `s`=lat-Latn. In Classical Latin, the paradigm was levelled back into the direct stem to change it to `robur`=lat-Latn instead of `robus`=lat-Latn.

The oak tree is named `robur`=lat-Latn from its reddish timber, and the "strength" and "hardness" meanings derive from the oak timber.
